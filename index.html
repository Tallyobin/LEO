LIGHTWEIGHT ENVIRONMENT OBSERVER

The LEO System helps keep you safe by observing your desired environment and sending alerts right away! It uses a camera, sensors that detect movement and sound, and a speaker to spot unwanted visitors, take pictures, and play warning sounds. LEO is easy to control and adjust to fit your security needs!
